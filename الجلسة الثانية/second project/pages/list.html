How to make Hot Chocolate

To make hot chocolate, you'll need the following ingredients:

  • 2 cups of milk
  • 2 tablespoons of unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 tablespoons of granulated sugar (adjust according to your taste)
  • 1/4 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • Whipped cream or marshmallows (optional, for topping)

Here's a simple recipe to make hot chocolate:

  1. In a small saucepan, heat the milk over medium heat until it starts to steam. Be careful not to let it boil.
    • the milk should be unexpierd
    • you can use powder milk also
  2. In a separate bowl, mix the cocoa powder and sugar together until well combined.
  3. Gradually add the cocoa powder mixture to the saucepan with the milk, whisking constantly to prevent any lumps from forming.
  4. Continue to heat the mixture, stirring occasionally, until it reaches your desired temperature and the cocoa powder and sugar are fully dissolved.
  5. Remove the saucepan from the heat and stir in the vanilla extract.
  6. Pour the hot chocolate into mugs and top with whipped cream or marshmallows if desired.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy!
